# Resilience and observability

Distributed systems fail partially. Bound failures with deadlines, idempotent retries, circuit breakers, bulkheads, and admission control. Every remote call needs a timeout and every queue needs a capacity limit.

Use metrics to detect trends, structured logs to explain events, and traces to follow requests across services. Define service objectives from user-visible outcomes such as successful requests under a latency threshold.

Monitor saturation, error-budget burn, dependency latency, retry amplification, and queue age. Recovery drills should prove that alerts are actionable and documented procedures still work.
